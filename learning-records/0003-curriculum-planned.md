# Curriculum planned — 3 years, 120 units

Built the full [[CURRICULUM.md]] and student-facing `reference/curriculum.html`: 3 years × 4
quarters × ~10 units. Year 1 = markets + math bridge + honest ML (data/labeling); Year 2 =
features/ensembles, **backtesting & overfitting** (the differentiator), microstructure, deep
learning/NLP; Year 3 = portfolio construction/risk, execution/RL, research infra + latency
awareness, and interview mastery + capstone.

Pacing model: **unit ≠ day** (~1.5–2 h/day, ~2,000 h over 3 years; reading ~5%, labs ~43%).
Interview prep (mental math, brainteasers, LeetCode) runs weekly from Year 1, concentrated in
Y3 Q4. Quarterly currency rule keeps the frontier (TFMs, deep LOB, RL) fresh while the canon
(Shreve, AFML, Grinold-Kahn, Almgren-Chriss, Cont) stays stable.

Additions beyond the user's starting list: markets-from-zero (Y1 Q1), portfolio construction &
risk (Y3 Q1), and research infrastructure / reproducibility — all essential for a working QR and
otherwise missing. Published Units 001–005 as real lessons + the Lesson-004 lab to make the
workspace immediately usable.
